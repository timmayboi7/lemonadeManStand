On the First Draft

The first draft is not the book. It is the conversation you have with yourself about what the book might become. It is full of wrong turns, dead ends, and passages that exist only to help you understand what you are trying to say.

Do not show the first draft to anyone. It is not ready. It is not meant to be ready. It is meant to be explored, expanded, and eventually, edited into something that resembles a story.
